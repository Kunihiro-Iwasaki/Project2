# Project2

## Title
### 70% of New York Buildings Violate Public Space Regulations
## Short description of what I aimed to accomplish
### When walking around Manhattan, you’ll come across public spaces marked with “OPEN TO PUBLIC” signs. In fact, these public spaces were developed by building owners in exchange for permission to construct larger buildings. A total of 392 buildings have developed such spaces, resulting in approximately 600 locations throughout New York City.
Are building owners properly operating and maintaining these public spaces after receiving their bonuses? An analysis of multiple New York City open data sets revealed that 70 percent of the 392 buildings had been cited for violations by city authorities.
